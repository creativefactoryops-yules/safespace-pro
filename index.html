IR Camera Detector
📷
Turn off all lights for best results

⚠️ Detection Guide

  • Turn off ALL room lights completely
  • IR LEDs appear as bright purple/white dots
  • Move slowly and scan from multiple angles
  • Check vents, smoke detectors, outlets first
  • Real IR lights stay constant, not flickering

What to Look For

  • Small bright spots visible only on camera
  • Purple or white glowing points
  • Consistent brightness from all angles
  • Multiple small lights in cluster (camera array)
Lens Reflection Finder
🔍
Turn on your flashlight

⚠️ Detection Guide

  • Turn ON phone flashlight for best results
  • Hold phone at 45° angle to surfaces
  • Camera lenses create circular bright spots
  • Move slowly and watch for consistent reflections
  • Check same spot from multiple angles

Priority Check Locations

  • Smoke detectors (most common hiding spot)
  • Digital clocks and alarm clocks
  • USB chargers and power adapters
  • Picture frames facing sensitive areas
  • Air vents and grilles
  • Decorative items on shelves
Audio Spectrum Analyzer

⚠️ Detection Guide

  • Turn off music, TV, and other noise sources
  • Some bugs emit ultrasonic frequencies (18-22 kHz)
  • Look for consistent high-frequency patterns
  • Move slowly around room while monitoring
  • Spikes in specific frequencies may indicate devices

Frequency Guide

  • 0-4 kHz: Human voice and ambient noise
  • 4-10 kHz: Normal environmental sounds
  • 10-18 kHz: Edge of human hearing
  • 18-22 kHz: Ultrasonic range (bugs/devices)
  • Watch for consistent narrow-band signals
Light Flicker Detector
💡
Detect invisible pulsing lights

⚠️ Detection Guide

  • Some IR cameras pulse at specific frequencies
  • Human eyes can't see this, but cameras can
  • Point camera at suspected devices
  • Look for rhythmic brightness changes
  • Common frequencies: 30Hz, 50Hz, 60Hz

What Flicker Means

  • Normal lights: Minimal flicker or 50/60Hz (power)
  • LED lights: May show high-frequency flicker
  • IR cameras: Often pulse at 30-60Hz
  • Suspicious: Consistent non-standard frequencies
